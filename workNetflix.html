Research and problem

Having a look at the Netflix platform, I realized that some new functions could be added.
Below you can find this functions:

1) Recommend movies and tv series to friends.

2) Watch a film o a tv series simultaneusly with other people.

3) Consult other countries’ movie and series catalogue.



Next, I made some interviews and carried out some surveys to know users’
opinions about the new functions. Unanimously, recommending movies
and tv series to other users was the winner.
