User persona

Competitive Analysis

The apps that I found and analyzed were the following:

  • Therapy chat
  • Wysa
  • Sanvello
  • Mind diagnostic
  • Meyo
  • Focusly

All these applications do not have the chat option, many exercises are paid
and the breathing option does not include relaxing music or different breathing options.

Solution

APP that helps people suffering from anxiety or panic attacks. Its main function is to
control the breathing rhythm (individual and group breathing exercises) and
to offer support through a chat (individual, group and professional chat).

User flows

Site map

On the basis of the person's needs and the respective solutions
to their problems and pain-points, I elaborated the site map.

Wireframes

To ensure that the application was intuitive to use, I tested the prototype with several users.
I found some options confusing, such as the buttons to choose the type of breath and the option to go to my existing conversations.
Finally, with several rounds of testing, the app seemed intuitive and easy to use.
